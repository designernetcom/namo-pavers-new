Innovative Paving Solutions for Every Space

Since 2008, Namo Pavers and Designer Tiles has been a trusted leader in the manufacture and supply of high-quality paver blocks, tiles, and cobblestones. Based in Pune, Maharashtra, we take pride in delivering durable and aesthetically pleasing products that elevate any space—be it residential, commercial, or industrial.

With a wide selection of paver blocks in various dimensions and finishes, including matt and designer options, we cater to diverse needs and tastes. Our commitment to quality, strength, and exceptional service has established us as a reliable partner in flooring and outdoor solutions. Under the leadership of Mr. Ritesh Jain, we continue to innovate and uphold the highest standards of craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.
