Garden/ Grass Paver Block

Garden/ Grass Paver Block

Garden or grass paver blocks are eco-friendly and functional paving solutions designed to allow vegetation to grow through their openings. These blocks not only provide a visually pleasing appearance but also enhance soil permeability, making them an excellent choice for gardens, driveways, pathways, and parking areas.

Thickness :

10 to 30 mm

Applications :

Gardens and Lawns, Public Parks.

Explore Different Types

01

Honeycomb Paver Blocks

Features hexagonal openings resembling a honeycomb. Ideal for gardens and light traffic areas where grass coverage is a priority.

02

Circular Grass Paver Blocks

Round openings within a square or rectangular base. Suitable for gardens, pathways, and decorative outdoor spaces.

03

Grid or Cell-Type Grass
Paver Blocks

Uniform grid-like pattern for optimal grass coverage. Commonly used in parking lots, driveways, and large garden areas.
